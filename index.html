VI(H)SIBLES.

FINAL MAJOR PROJECT • 2025

INTERACTIVE INSTALLATION ABOUT THE SOCIAL STIGMA AROUND HIV+ PEOPLE IN CHILE.

VI(H)SIBLES IS A PROJECT THAT AIMS TO RAISE AWARENESS ABOUT THE CHALLENGES FACED BY HIV-POSITIVE INDIVIDUALS IN CHILE, FOCUSING ON THE VOICES THAT HAVE BEEN MARGINALIZED AND OFTEN IGNORED IN SOCIETY.

REAL TIME VISUALS AND SPACIAL DESIGN BASED COMPLETELY ON REAL TESTIMONIES OF PEOPLE LIVING WITH HIV IN CHILE, MAKING THEM AN IMPORTANT PART OF THE NARRATIVE

WITH THE USE OF DISTANCE AND PROXIMITY SENSORS CONNECTED THROUGH ARDUINO AND DATA SENT TO TOUCHDESIGNER, THE SPECTATOR BECOMES A PART OF THE EXPERIENCE, GETTING DIRECTLY INVOLVED WITH THE NARRATIVE.

PROJECT IN CONTINUOUS DEVELOPMENT AND EVOLUTION. WITH THE MAIN OBJECTIVE OF MAKING VISIBLE THOSE VOICES THAT HAVE BEEN HISTORICALLY SILENCED.
